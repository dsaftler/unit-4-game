Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crysals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score, but the value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.

Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal - and the random number - will change.
