Math-magical Crystals!

You will be given a random number to match at the start of the game.

Starting from 0, you can win the game by adding just enough points to match the random number.

There are four crysals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total, but the value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Then you'll have to figure out how to use them to add up to your match without going over.

On each new game, the values of the random number and each crystal will change.
