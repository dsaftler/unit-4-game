Crystal Math-Magic!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

You win the game by matching your total points to the random number.

There are four crysals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total, but the value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. You'll have to figure those out to match without going over.

Each time the game starts, the values of the random number and each crystal will change.
